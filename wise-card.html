❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Wise Business card really free?
Yes — the first Wise Business debit card is included free with your account. Replacement cards cost €5. Additional cards for team members are available at a small cost. There are no annual card fees and no monthly maintenance charges.
Are there ATM withdrawal fees with Wise card?
Wise Business provides 2 free ATM withdrawals per month (up to €200 total). After that, a small fee applies (typically 1.75% + €0.50 per withdrawal). For most business use cases — paying suppliers and online subscriptions — ATM withdrawals are rarely needed.
Can I use the Wise card for Google Ads, Meta Ads, and software subscriptions?
Yes. The Wise Business debit card works for all online payments including Google Ads, Meta/Facebook Ads, AWS, Stripe, SaaS subscriptions, and any other online service. USD-billed services are charged at the real EUR/USD rate — no 3% foreign fee like most bank cards.
How quickly does the Wise card arrive?
Physical Wise Business cards typically arrive within 5–10 business days in Belgium, Germany, and France. You can also create virtual cards instantly for immediate use in online payments while waiting for the physical card.
