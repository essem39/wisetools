Text Analysis for EU Business Documents

Our text analyzer gives you instant statistics on any text — from a short email to a full legal contract. It calculates word count, character count, sentence structure, reading time, and the most frequently used words. All processing happens in your browser — no text is ever sent to a server.

Word Count for Legal Contracts

In legal and business contexts, word count matters. Many translation agencies charge per word, so knowing the exact word count before requesting a translation saves time. EU contracts for public procurement often have length requirements. Employment contracts in Belgium, Germany, and France must cover specific topics — knowing the length helps ensure completeness.

Reading Time Estimation

The average adult reads 200–250 words per minute (silent reading). Our tool estimates reading time at 200 words/min. Speaking time is estimated at 130 words/min — useful for presentations, speeches, and video scripts. A standard A4 page contains approximately 500 words.

Character Count for EU Compliance Texts

Character limits matter for:

  • Meta descriptions (SEO): 150–160 characters recommended
  • Social media posts: Twitter/X 280 chars, LinkedIn 3,000 chars
  • SMS notifications: 160 characters per message
  • Product description fields: varies by platform
  • GDPR consent texts: must be clear and concise

Document Length Standards in EU Business

EU business documents follow different length conventions by type. A standard commercial invoice should be concise — typically 1 page. An NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) ranges from 1,000 to 3,000 words. A full service agreement typically runs 2,000–8,000 words. A GDPR Privacy Policy for an EU website should cover all required elements, typically 1,500–3,000 words.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my text stored or sent anywhere?
No. All text analysis runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your text never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers. You can safely paste confidential contracts, legal documents, or personal data — everything stays local.
How is reading time calculated?
Reading time is based on the average adult reading speed of 200 words per minute for silent reading. Speaking time uses 130 words per minute, which is a comfortable presentation pace. These are averages — actual times vary based on text complexity, individual reading speed, and purpose (skimming vs. careful reading).
How many words is a standard A4 page?
A standard A4 page with 12pt font, standard margins, and single spacing contains approximately 500 words. Double-spaced formatting reduces this to about 250 words per page. Legal documents often use 1.5 line spacing, yielding approximately 350 words per page.
What languages does the analyzer support?
The analyzer works with any language that uses spaces between words — including all EU languages (English, French, German, Dutch, Polish, Spanish, Italian, etc.). Character counting works for all Unicode characters. Word frequency analysis works best with Latin-script languages.
