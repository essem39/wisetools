EU VAT Validation — Complete Guide

The EU VAT validator checks whether a VAT identification number is currently active in the official VIES database maintained by the European Commission — the authoritative source for VAT number validation across all 27 EU member states.

Why VAT Validation Matters

  • Required for B2B cross-border EU transactions
  • Mandatory for reverse charge mechanism
  • Essential for EC Sales Lists
  • Necessary for OSS / IOSS reporting accuracy

Frequently Asked Questions

What is VIES VAT validation?
VIES (VAT Information Exchange System) is the official EU system operated by the European Commission. It allows businesses to verify whether a VAT number is valid and active across all EU member states in real time.
Is VAT number validation legally required?
Yes, for B2B cross-border transactions within the EU. Before applying the zero-rate (reverse charge), you must confirm the buyer has a valid VAT number and keep proof of verification for audit purposes.
Why does a valid VAT number sometimes fail?
VIES connects to national tax databases which are sometimes offline for maintenance. If a legitimate number returns an error, wait a few minutes and try again at ec.europa.eu/taxation_customs/vies/
